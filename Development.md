# Opticlass

**Smart School Management System**  
AI-powered school management system designed to support the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) in Kenya.

---

## Vision
Transforming education through intelligent technology.  

Opticlass simplifies school administration, empowers teachers with insights, personalizes student learning, and enables parents to be actively involved. It integrates cutting-edge technology with educational best practices to create a comprehensive ecosystem aligned with Kenyan schools’ needs.

---

## Development Roadmap

### Phase 1: MVP (Foundation)
**Goals:** Build the foundational system with essential school management features.  

**Key Features:**
- Student registration and profiles  
- Teacher and staff management  
- Basic timetable functionality  
- Grade and assessment tracking  
- User authentication and roles  

**Outcome:** A solid foundation that streamlines basic administrative tasks and provides a platform for future enhancements.

---

### Phase 2: Communication & Finance
**Goals:** Improve communication channels and financial management capabilities.  

**Key Features:**
- Parent-teacher communication portal  
- Fee management and payment tracking  
- School announcements and notifications  
- Attendance tracking with alerts  
- Financial reporting and analytics  

**Outcome:** Enhanced stakeholder communication and streamlined financial operations with automated reminders and reporting.

---

### Phase 3: AI & Smart Features
**Goals:** Implement AI-driven insights and personalized learning experiences.  

**Key Features:**
- AI-powered performance analytics  
- Personalized learning paths  
- Predictive analytics for at-risk students  
- Smart timetable optimization  
- Automated report generation  

**Outcome:** Data-driven insights that help identify student needs, optimize educational outcomes, and reduce administrative workload.

---

### Phase 4: Smart Integrations & Expansion
**Goals:** Expand capabilities and integrate with external systems.  

**Key Features:**
- Integration with government systems (e.g., KNEC)  
- Mobile app for parents and students  
- Library management system  
- Inventory and resource tracking  
- Multi-school management capabilities  

**Outcome:** A comprehensive ecosystem that connects all aspects of school management with external stakeholders and supports scaling to multiple institutions.
